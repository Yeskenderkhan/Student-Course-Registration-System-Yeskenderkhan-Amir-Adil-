Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Each course has a maximum capacity (e.g., 15 students). However, a course needs at least 60% capacity to be considered "Successfully Formed" (Green status). Before that, it is in "Pending" status (Yellow).

Currently, you need to contact the administrator to drop a course. We are working on adding this feature to your dashboard soon!
